Whitespark has been the citation specialist since 2010. Unlike all-in-one platforms like BrightLocal, Whitespark sells individual tools and services. You build your own stack. We tested their core products for 60 days to see if this approach still makes sense in 2026.

**Short answer:** Yes, for citation-heavy work. No, if you want everything in one place.

## What is Whitespark?

Whitespark offers individual local SEO tools and services:

- **Local Citation Finder** - discover where you're listed (and where you're not)
- **Local Rank Tracker** - monitor local pack and organic positions
- **Reputation Builder** - automate review collection
- **Local Platform** - basic listing management
- **Listings Service** - manual citation building by their team

Each product is priced separately. You can buy one or all.

## Key Products We Tested

### Local Citation Finder

The flagship product. Enter a business name and location, and Whitespark finds existing citations across thousands of directories.

**What it shows:**
- Where you're currently listed
- Citation accuracy (correct NAP vs. outdated info)
- Directories you're missing from
- Competitor citation profiles
- Opportunities by directory authority

**What works:** No tool finds more citations. The database is comprehensive, covering major aggregators, industry directories, and local sites. The competitor analysis reveals exactly where rivals are listed that you're not.

**What doesn't:** The interface is functional but dated. Exporting data for cleanup requires extra steps. And at $33-149/month depending on search volume, costs add up for agencies.

**Pricing:**
- Lite: $33/month (100 searches)
- Standard: $67/month (300 searches)
- Agency: $149/month (1,000 searches)

### Local Rank Tracker

Monitor local pack and organic rankings for target keywords across locations.

**Features include:**
- Daily rank updates
- Local pack position tracking
- Organic ranking monitoring
- Multiple location tracking
- Historical data and trends
- Email alerts for ranking changes

**What works:** Affordable entry point at $14/month for 25 keywords. Clean interface shows ranking trends clearly. Desktop and mobile tracking included.

**What doesn't:** No geo-grid visualization like Local Falcon. You track rankings from a single point, not across your service area. For service-area businesses, this is a significant limitation.

**Pricing:**
- Starter: $14/month (25 keywords)
- Pro: $49/month (100 keywords)
- Agency: $99/month (250 keywords)
- Enterprise: $200/month (500+ keywords)

### Reputation Builder

Automate review collection via email and SMS campaigns.

**Features include:**
- Email and SMS review requests
- Customizable templates
- Multi-platform support (Google, Facebook, industry sites)
- Negative review interception
- White-label for agencies

**What works:** The funnel approach asks customers about their experience first, then routes positive feedback to public review platforms. This improves your average rating naturally.

**What doesn't:** At $79/location/month, it's expensive compared to BrightLocal's bundled review features. Setup requires more effort than integrated platforms.

### Listings Service

Whitespark's team manually submits your business to directories.

**Pricing:**
- Citation building: $20-999 depending on number of sites
- Citation cleanup: Custom pricing
- One-time fee per project

**What works:** Manual submissions are higher quality than automated tools. Whitespark checks each submission rather than blasting information everywhere.

**What doesn't:** It's a service, not software. You pay per project, and ongoing management is your responsibility (or requires additional projects).

## The A-La-Carte Model: Pros and Cons

### Why It Works

**Pay for what you need.** If you only need citation research, you pay $33/month — not $150+ for an all-in-one platform. Agencies doing project-based citation work love this.

**Best-in-class tools.** When you specialize, you can be the best. Whitespark's Citation Finder is genuinely better than citation features in bundled platforms.

**Flexibility.** Mix Whitespark tools with other platforms. Use their Citation Finder with BrightLocal for rank tracking. Build your ideal stack.

### Why It Doesn't

**Tool fatigue.** Managing 3-4 separate subscriptions, logins, and interfaces adds friction. All-in-one platforms are simply more convenient.

**Costs add up.** Citation Finder ($67) + Rank Tracker ($49) + Reputation Builder ($79) = $195/month. BrightLocal might be cheaper for the same capabilities.

**No unified dashboard.** You can't see citations, rankings, and reviews in one view. Reporting requires pulling data from multiple sources.

## FAQ

### Is Whitespark better than BrightLocal?

For citations specifically, yes. Whitespark's Citation Finder is more comprehensive. For all-in-one local SEO, BrightLocal is more convenient. Many professionals use both.

### Does Whitespark have a free trial?

Most products don't offer free trials. The Local Platform has a 7-day trial. Citation Finder and Rank Tracker require paid subscriptions from day one.

### How much does Whitespark cost total?

Depends on what you need. Citation Finder alone is $33/month. A full stack (Citation Finder + Rank Tracker + Reputation Builder) runs $195+/month per location.

### Is Whitespark good for agencies?

Yes — the a-la-carte model works well for project-based agency work. Buy Citation Finder access, run audits for clients, build citations with the Listings Service. No ongoing subscription between projects.

### Can Whitespark replace BrightLocal?

Technically yes, but with more complexity. You'd need Citation Finder + Rank Tracker + Reputation Builder + manual GBP management. Most find BrightLocal more convenient for full-service local SEO.
