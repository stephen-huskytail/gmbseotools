Local Falcon pioneered geo-grid rank tracking for local search. Instead of showing your rank from one location, it shows where you rank at every point across your service area. After 60 days of testing across multiple client accounts, here's whether it's worth adding to your stack.

**Short answer:** Yes, if you serve a geographic area. No, if you only need basic keyword tracking.

## What is Local Falcon?

Local Falcon visualizes your local pack rankings across a geographic grid. Traditional rank trackers tell you "you rank #3 for 'plumber near me.'" Local Falcon tells you "you rank #1 downtown, #3 in the suburbs, and #7 at the edge of your service area."

This matters because local rankings vary by searcher location. A business might dominate near their address but be invisible 10 miles away. Without geo-grid tracking, you'd never know.

## How Geo-Grid Tracking Works

You enter your business, target keyword, and desired grid size (3x3, 5x5, up to 21x21). Local Falcon checks your local pack ranking at each point on the grid.

**The result:** A visual heatmap showing where you rank well (green), moderately (yellow), and poorly (red).

**Grid sizes and credits:**
- 3x3 grid = 9 points = 9 credits per scan
- 5x5 grid = 25 points = 25 credits per scan
- 7x7 grid = 49 points = 49 credits per scan
- 21x21 grid = 441 points = 441 credits per scan

More points = more detailed visualization = more credits consumed.

Track up to 3 competitors on the same grid.

**What works:** See exactly where competitors dominate vs. where you have opportunity. The "Share of Voice" metric shows what percentage of the grid you own vs. competitors.

**What doesn't:** Each competitor on the grid consumes additional credits. A 5x5 grid with 3 competitors = 100 credits per scan (25 x 4 businesses).

### Trending & Historical Data

Local Falcon stores historical scans so you can track progress over time.

**What works:** Prove ROI by showing ranking improvements across the service area. Useful for monthly client reports.

**What doesn't:** You have to remember to run scans consistently. No automated scheduling on standard plans.

## FAQ

### Do I need geo-grid rank tracking?

If you serve a geographic area (not just one address), yes. Basic rank tracking shows your position from one point. Geo-grid shows where you rank everywhere across your service area — essential for understanding true visibility.

### How many credits do I need?

Depends on tracking frequency and grid size. A 5x5 grid uses 25 credits per scan. Tracking 10 keywords weekly = 1,000 credits/month. The Starter plan (7,500 credits) handles most single-location needs.

### Does Local Falcon replace BrightLocal?

No — they're complementary. Local Falcon is specialized for geo-grid tracking. BrightLocal handles citations, reviews, and GBP management. Most serious local SEOs use both.

### Is Local Falcon worth it for one location?

Probably yes if you serve a service area. Even single-location businesses serve geographic markets. Understanding where you rank (and don't) across that area is valuable.

### What's the best Local Falcon alternative?

BrightLocal includes basic geo-grid tracking (5 keywords). Places Scout is a direct competitor. For specialized geo-grid tracking, Local Falcon remains the market leader.

### Can I automate Local Falcon scans?

Not natively on standard plans. You must manually trigger each scan. Some agencies use API access or third-party automation for regular tracking.
