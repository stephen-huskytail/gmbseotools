## FAQ

### Which GMB tool is best for a single location?

**Localo** ($29/mo) offers the best value for single locations. It covers GBP posting, basic rank tracking, and competitor analysis without enterprise complexity. BrightLocal is better but costs more.

### Do I need geo-grid rank tracking?

If you serve a geographic area (not just one address), yes. Basic rank tracking shows your position for a single location. Geo-grid tracking (Local Falcon) shows where you rank at every point across your service area — essential for understanding true visibility.

### What's the difference between BrightLocal and Whitespark?

- **BrightLocal** is an all-in-one platform (audits, tracking, citations, reviews)
- **Whitespark** sells individual tools a-la-carte (citation finder, rank tracker, etc.)

Choose BrightLocal for convenience, Whitespark if you only need citation-specific tools.

### How much should I budget for local SEO tools?

- **Basic (single location):** $30-50/month (Localo or basic Whitespark tools)
- **Intermediate:** $100-200/month (BrightLocal + Local Falcon)
- **Agency:** $300-500+/month depending on location count

### Are citations still important in 2026?

Yes, but less than before. Consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) across major directories still matters for trust signals. But reviews and GBP optimization have more ranking impact. Prioritize citation cleanup over aggressive building.

### Can AI tools fully automate GBP management?

Not fully. Tools like Merchynt's Paige AI can automate posting and draft review responses, but human review is still essential. AI-generated content needs editing, and review responses should be personalized for significant customer interactions.
